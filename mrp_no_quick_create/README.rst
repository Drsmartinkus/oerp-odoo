Stock - No quick create on form
###############################

Disables quick create option for some fields on MRP form views as
those can be used when not needed and usually just gets in a way.

On manufacturing order:

* Product
* Responsible
* Bill of Material
* Component

On Bill of Material:

* Product
* Product Variant
* Component
